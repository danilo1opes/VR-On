Common Questions.

What form of payment do you accept?
We accept credit cards with the following brands: Visa, MasterCard, ELO and American Express. For cash payments we also accept PIX and Boleto through PagSeguro.
Does insurance cover theft of VR glasses?
Yes, our insurance plan offers protection against qualified theft of your VR headset, with support for quick device replacement.
Are VR glasses compatible with game consoles?
Yes, our VR glasses are compatible with multiple platforms, including popular consoles like PlayStation and Xbox.
How do I set up the VR headset for the first time?
Connect the device to your computer or console, install the necessary software, and follow the on-screen instructions for calibration and adjustment.
Is it safe for children to use VR glasses?
We recommend that children use the VR headset under adult supervision and for limited periods of time.
How do I clean and care for my VR glasses?
To clean your VR headset, use a soft microfiber cloth to wipe the lenses. Avoid using abrasive cleaning products that could damage components.
What are the minimum system requirements to use the VR headset?
We recommend a computer with a high-end processor, at least 8GB of RAM, a VR-compatible graphics card, and USB 3.0 ports.
